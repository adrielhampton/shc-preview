Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Social Housing California is committed to protecting the privacy of people who visit this site and sign up for our campaign updates.

Information We Collect

When you sign up through our contact form, we collect your name, email address, and phone number. This information is used solely to send you updates about the Social Housing California campaign.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to send campaign updates, event announcements, and action alerts related to social housing in California. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Third-Party Services

Our sign-up form is powered by 123ContactForm. Please review their privacy policy for information on how form data is processed and stored.

Cookies

This site uses minimal analytics to understand how visitors find and use the site. No personally identifiable information is collected through analytics.

Contact

Questions about this privacy policy? Email us at info@socialhousingca.org.
